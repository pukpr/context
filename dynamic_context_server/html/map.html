Environmental models can either be general in terms of their suitability (such as water properties or a standard atmosphere), or specific to a geospatial location, which can further include daily or seasonal effects.

The locations are accessible through the semantic drop-down list or through indexed search terms with keystroke lookahead.

The map button indicates the location of the model or data, and the available button links to the modeling domain. After submitted, click on marker to magnify the scale.
