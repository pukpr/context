This category links to models of fossil fuel usage and potential impacts on climate change.

The models are useful for making projections but as much of the data can not be verified as controlled experiments, the results are somewhat speculative.

A detailed description of the fossil fuel resource models are available via the online book The Oil Conundrum (also at Google Books).

Both sets are discussed at Context/Earth

The CSALT model describes the global average temperature anomaly as studied in climate change science.

The details are described here:

  1. Climate Variability and Inferring Global Warming
  2. CSALT Model

These are all experimental and in process.
